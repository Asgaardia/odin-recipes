Caramel Almond Butter Cookies

Description

You can make these delicious, simple cookies with regular almond butter if you want, but I always love having the extra layer of flavor that comes from using Maranatha caramel almond butter.

These cookies tend to spread at higher elevations, so it would be best to add a little extra flour.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup Maranatha caramel almond butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large bowl, add the butter, almond butter, and both types of sugar. Cream together until well blended. Beat in the egg.
  3. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture.
  4. Combine thoroughly, then chill the batter in the fridge for an hour.
  5. Roll into 1 inch balls and place on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a spoon and press an almond in the top.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
