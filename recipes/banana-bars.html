Banana Bars

Description

These soft and chewy banana bars, loaded with decadent chocolate and white chocolate chips, are delicious any time of the day.

If you are somehow able to resist eating them all right away, they freeze well for up to three months.

Ingredients

  • 7 1/2 very ripe bananas (about 3 1/3 cups)
  • 1 1/8 cups brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons oil
  • 6 tablespoons milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup white chocolate chips, divided
  • 3/4 cup mini chocolate chips, divided

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Spray two 9x13 metal pans with non-stick spray.
  2. Peel bananas and mash well. Stir in brown sugar, oil, milk, and eggs until well combined.
  3. Add in dry ingredients and stir. Fold in half of the chocolate and white chocolate chips.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pans and sprinkle the remaining chocolate and white chocolate chips on top.
  5. Bake 17-22 minutes, until a wooden toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool completely and then cut into 2-3 inch squares. Makes about 48 bars.
